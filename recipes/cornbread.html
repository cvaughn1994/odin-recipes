Karah's Cornbread

Description

This is a great cornbread recipe my wife uses. We enjoy pairing this with almost any dinner, but pinto beans is our most common complimentary dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 1/4 Cup of Coconut Oil
  • 1 1/2 Cup of Unsweetened Almond Milk
  • 2 Cups All-Purpose Cornmeal

Steps

  1. Grease Pan and Preheat Oven to 450
  2. Put Empty Pan in Oven for 5 Minutes
  3. Whisk Egg then Add the Other Ingredients
  4. Stir and Add to Pan
  5. Bake for 20-25 Minutes
