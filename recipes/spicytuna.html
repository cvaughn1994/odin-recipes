Spicy Tuna Salad

Description

This is my favorite way to prepare Spicy Tuna Salad. It's a recipe given to me by a friend, and I've been enjoying it for several years now. The spiciness can be adjusted to your liking, but I have a low threshold for spicy. This tuna salad goes great on sandwiches, crackers, or fresh, cored tomatoes and bell peppers.

Ingredients

  • 4 Small Cans of Tuna
  • Spoonful or 2 of Mayo
  • Splash of Olive Oil
  • 2 Diced Jalapenos
  • 1 Diced Cucumber
  • Dash of Salt and Pepper
  • 2 Pinches of Oregano
  • 1 Lime Fully Squeezed
  • Tablespoon of Relish
  • 4 Chopped Boiled Eggs

Steps

  1. Boil 4 Eggs
  2. Dice Jalapenos and Cucumber
  3. Drain Tuna Cans
  4. Mix All Ingredients in Large Bowl
  5. Let Chill for 1 Hour
  6. Serve!
